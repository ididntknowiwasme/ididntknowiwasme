More About Us

Why We Exist

I Didn't Know I Was Me exists to guide people in discovering their true identity in Christ — and help them walk boldly in who God says they are.

Join Our Community Learn More

Before finding identity in Christ, many of us spent years searching in places that could never truly satisfy. Just as a product's full potential is known only by its manufacturer, only God fully knows who we are meant to be. Through His Word, we help people uncover and walk in their God-ordained purpose — free from the labels and limitations of the world.

The goal is not to be who the world says we are, but to become who God says we are. We are passionate about guiding people — especially young believers — toward discovering their God-given identity and purpose through biblical study, discipleship, and authentic community.

Our Three Pillars

→

Identity in Christ

True identity can only be found in God. We help people uncover and embrace their God-given identity and purpose through His Word.

→

Discipleship & Growth

→

No one is meant to walk this journey alone. We are a family of believers, creating a safe space to be vulnerable, encouraged, and strengthened in faith.
